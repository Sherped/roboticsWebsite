Description

CRC Robotics was founded in 2001 by a group of young professionals and teachers, fueled by their passion for robotics and education. Unsatisfied with the robotics competitions available for high schools and CEGEPs in Canada, they created an annual competition linking science, mathematics, computers, arts, and languages. Since the 2021-2022 season, CRC Robotics has also acquired a junior division, in which elementary schools across Quebec can compete!
